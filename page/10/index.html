Prologue

这个世界上到底都在发生着什么？
zky视角 ——被偷偷翻看的日记

Mon

又是一个作为执政凶兽的周一呢……这么奇怪的职务名称是怎么想出来的，我怎么说也是个人类啊！不管了，又是忙碌的一天，Hikari应该已经做好早饭了，嗯……起床太早会吃不下早饭呢……我就再睡亿……小会儿……

“zky？你今天也要睡到下午吗？”我睁开眼睛，Hikari眨着眼从门缝里探出头来，手上端着香喷喷的早饭。

“哦，我这就起来，还是不要再迟到了吧。”我跳下床，看了一眼钟，接过早饭就大口吃了起来，“下次可以多放一点芝士。”

“好的，我记住了。”

……

“啊不好意思我知道我又迟到了，让你们久等了，下次我定两个闹钟……”我匆匆忙忙冲进发布会场上，看着下面密密麻麻的相机，“今天我们的主题是USLT等级考试，我们开始吧。USLT已经进行了两个月，共有约30000人次参加，其中仅不足100人次考试等级大于等于4，所有考试的通过率达到30%，可见其严格程度。不过，这样的数据已经足以让我们看出来自世界各地参赛者的热情，相信下一个考试季我们会迎来更多身怀绝技的人才。另外，我亲自签署的《关于USLT考试季期间三界通行许可调整》也已经完全实行，目前看来……”

“龙王大人，讲得还算可以吧？”我走出通道，龙王大人正在那里等我，“至少没像上次那样不知道讲啥。”

“Still a bit off.”

“A bit of what?”

“off, o-f-f-”

“But…why?”

“新闻发布会不是你这么说话的，别人可没有这么口语化，都官方正式多了，你又没写稿子吧，一听就是临场发挥的。嗯，你还又迟到了。”

“这个是我的特色啦~这么轻松的发布会谁不喜欢？”

“迟到也是你的特色吗？”

“啊这…”

……

要是我写稿子的话我就没法享受这样的下午了，听了一个下午音乐的我这样想着……

Tue

附身鸟与龙程度的能力，还是Rinri的妹妹？我觉得这样的能力很危险啊，我要让她加入SSC，这样就可以监视她了，SSC的追踪器，声称是只监测位置信息，实际上……我可没有这么傻，早就安装了各种监控，包括魔法能力，周围人物和AI自动判别是否故意伤害等。算了，让我假装不知道，先去见一见这个孩子吧。

“Rinri，你好呀……你是……？”

“你好，我叫Rinko，是Rinri的妹妹。请问你找他有什么事吗？”她一开口，就是淳朴踏实的感觉。

“哈哈，怪不得你还蛮像他的呢！没事，不用打扰你哥哥了，我只是来买点面包的。”她真的是这样淳朴善良的农村女孩吗？如果是这样，那我也不必纠结这些问题了，“这么久我都不知道Rinri还有个这么可爱的妹妹呢，那这么说，你也会使用水和火的强力攻击咯？”

“不…我不会…我不像Rinri哥哥那么厉害……”她说着微微低下头，脸颊上微微有些泛红。

“那你应该有一些别的能力吧？”

“也许算是吧…我可以和鸟类有独特的感应。”

“哦，这么有意思？可以展示一下吗？”

Rinko在转眼间消失了，一只棕色的小麻雀扑闪着翅膀飞进了小店里，小心翼翼地落在了我的肩膀上，黑溜溜的眼睛看着我，扭扭头，啾啾叫了两声，又飞走了，Rinko又重新出现在了原地。

“哇！太棒了，我从来没有见过这样的能力。我也介绍一下自己吧，我是zky，我觉得你应该知道我的，现在我邀请你加入SSC，作为SSC的成员，你的义务是以自己的能力保护世界，你不需要一定是最强，只要你有独特的能力，在有需要的地方与其他SSC成员合作，你就是这个世界独一无二的守护者。而这项荣誉的报酬也是十分丰厚的：每个月固定20000元的奖金。”

“我…还是不想加入，对不起zky，我只想过我自己安静的生活，我承担不了这样的责任。”Rinko咬着嘴唇，十分为难地说出拒绝。

“没关系的，在这个世界每个人都可以做自己最想做的事情。嗯，这里可以结账吗？”我抓着五大袋面包从货架前走了回来。

“嗯，可以的，但是zky你就别付钱了吧，你来这里是我们的荣幸。”

“不用这样啦，我都是老顾客了，”我好不容易腾出手掏出钱，轻轻放在桌上，“这是个令人惊叹的城市，每一处都是奇观，每一秒都是艺术，玩的开心，顺便替我向Rinri问个好哦。”

“嗯好的，谢谢光临！”

Wed

“今天我们开个Alternative Universe玩真人Among Us吧？”

“漂亮，我早就想让你看看我思维敏捷逻辑清晰勇敢智慧敏锐果断的zky是如何在这种游戏里面杀人不眨眼，血溅当场不留线索，在台前幕后操纵全局主宰比赛的！”

龙王大人不屑地看了看我：“那我们开始了。”

Round1

“我知道了，就是Naza！一定是这样的，我们赶快处决她！”

“不行啊，必须要死一个人才能处决杀手。”

“那…为了我们的胜利，为了所有玩家，龙王大人，你来击杀我，然后所有人投票给Naza，你们就可以赢了！”我心中的英雄气概油然而生，就算我不是游戏的胜利者，我也会被认为是真正的胜利者，“龙王大人，动手吧，别急，慢慢玩死才刺激啊……”

“游戏结束，Naza不是杀手！”

“Cao，失误！真的是失误！”我一头撞在墙上哭笑不得。

Round2

“呵呵这样没有人能发现我杀人了~”我心中窃喜。

“所有人展示自己还可以使用魔法，以证明你们今天没有使用过魔法杀人。”

“啊这…这也行？！”

“就是zky，处决了！”

Round3

“这次嫁祸给Ookami，绝对没有问题了。”我咬紧牙关，几乎恼羞成怒。

“不对！Ookami身高只有1.6m，不可能完成这个操作，反而zky身高是1.75m，完全符合击杀手段！”

“不是吧！”

“处决！”

Thu

但是有时，事情却不是那么轻松的，甚至是生死攸关的。没办法，站在什么位置上，就要面对什么样的问题，欲戴王冠，必承其重。

“我们要不要联系她？Kero完全有能力把她击杀。”

“不必，你觉得她会看不出来吗？你看这里，空间缺失度已经到了85%，任何生物都应该能刚知道了，看着，”Hypret指着屏幕，“她开了光之护盾，这恐怕是我能想到的最精明的选择，如果略有急迫很可能会选择集中型攻击，那可麻烦了，这几百人估计就没法活下来了。”

“也是，再说以Kero的实力，他完全在我们的掌控之下。”

“你负责在Booktalk期间全程监视，一旦有情况直接把Warning信号报给我，我去找Segic和zhukyRLG回来。”

……

“什么？！她直接去睡觉了？”

“不大聪明，暗元素生物在黑夜里每一刻实力都在指数级增强，我估计就在午夜……Kero已经是凶兽级了，怎么可以这么掉以轻心？”

“我去找她吧，看在这么几百人都已经命悬一线的份上！”

“你真的想吗？你这么做不会让她感觉你什么事情都要管着她吗？”

“那就拿几百条人命做代价？”

“zky，认真地回答我，你有可能真的在乎这么几百个人吗？”

“……我明白了”

午夜！

微微闪着银白色光芒的护盾像是断路的电灯，无力地闪烁了几下就消失了，黑暗笼罩了整个流金乡，暗之精灵如风一般掠过村庄，恐惧与虚无的异常感受唤醒了几乎每一个人，可他们已经来不及考虑了……

因为他们已经活不过那一刻了！

无数暗之精灵如同恶魔的星辰在原地爆裂，整个村庄陷入了死寂，爆裂吸收的能量已经让整个村庄变为极寒地狱。Hikari的光芒瞬间散尽，所幸她的能量源于龙王大人，在下一秒就已经重生。

空气几乎要冻住整个肺部，脚下的地面是从未感受过的寒冷，冰霜刺在脚掌上，Hikari等在原地，警觉地看着四周，小心地向前迈出一步，脚下的地面轰然爆破，暗之精灵从地下飞出，Kero，终于现身了，他手握着归零球，“让它直接打碎你的灵魂，会是什么感觉呢？”

“Teleport！”Hikari睁开眼睛，我把她推到一旁，闪过冲来的降维光束，Hypret展开结界，挡住第一波攻击。

“Daybreak！”Segic双手对准冲来的暗之精灵，它们吸收了火花的能量后纷纷快速衰变，消失在空中。zhukyRLG扔出一张Spell Entry，棱角贴着Kero的脸划过。

“是你们？！”Kero消失在夜空中，无迹可寻……

……

“最后一次机会，如果再失败一次的话……”Kero的脑海中隐隐响起声音，什么时候，难道被别人操控了？

Fri

又要去新闻发布会了，Diary昨天半夜给我好多新闻社的问题，今天必须要解答一下。

“今天我只迟到了一分钟，可喜可贺啊，我们先来讨论一下……”我的目光从闪光灯前移动到了窗外，天色阴沉了下来，黑雾隐天蔽日，在那无边的混沌中，一阵强大的虚无冲击打破了时空的平稳，这是对执政凶兽赤裸裸的挑衅，Kero，今天就是你被退治的日子。

“Sword of Souls, Summ…”手中突然出现的剑喷发出看一眼就能致盲的极限光束，可惜我的魔法能量完全无法维持，火花四散，手中只剩下空洞的剑柄。我苦笑一声，“这大概是你们第一次看到Final zky吧。”

红蓝两色的能量场在我的双手间聚集，我的瞳孔渐渐显出颜色，两种新的能量正在融入我的身体。

“Spell Entry No. 14 Sun Ray-light”，一张Spell Entry从我手中飞出，砸碎了窗户飞向远处，突然爆发出太阳般的夺目金光，退散了黑雾，没等Kero的身形在褪去的光芒中显现清楚，我轻轻一转手腕，又是一张Spell Entry顺势飞出，我翻出窗户，抬起SB炮，震动着空气的飞弹直冲云霄，每一颗都是致命的高能。Entry擦着Kero的脸颊飞过，他目光冷峻地看着我，一打响指，空中的暗之精灵一个个地瞄准着我俯冲下来，我左右闪避，破碎球在我脚边炸开飞石，艰难的一个滚翻闪过极速撞来的归零球，却还是被和暗之精灵一起突袭的黑暗球击中，若不是我也是暗元素生物，这一击已经可以把我秒杀了。那张飞过的Entry悬停在空中，突然放出四道激光集中在Kero身上，他转身放出黑雾阻挡，而我已经飞冲上前，“Summon: The Sword of OP - Galaxy Level” (Tip:OP之剑有Planet, Solar, Galaxy, Universe, Multiverse, Destoryer 6个等级)

贯穿星系的极光将Kero击成两段，剑柄在手中发烫，火花映红了我的眼瞳。

“就这？”Kero收回黑雾，自身又一次完好如初，他的脸上，是杀手的笑意，只不过，就在那一刻，他已经被撕成无数的碎片，飘散在空中。

“Gekrot，干得漂亮！”我从空中落下，满意地点了点头，一眨眼，在我的身旁，三只黑色的小球慢悠悠地旋转着，“嗯？这还是算我的人头？”。

X-Gekrot收起黄色的灵魂能量，“无所谓，我也不能复制能力。”

Sat

不宅在家里怎么是个宅男？

吸收了太多的暗之能量和“零”，现在精神有点不正常……

我和过去的凶兽不同，我关爱这个世界，我从未为了自己的强化而屠杀。

历史的车轮滚滚而去，没有凶兽可以逃过被新的凶兽杀死，然后被当做饲料吃掉的命运。

要想活下去，只能快速变强自己，那就是，选择屠杀。

若我不再需要朋友，Ninetail，Hikari，Rinri兄妹，Doragon…不会有一人幸存，甚至…我不敢想下去了。

In the world, is kill or be killed!

拿不起屠刀的手，就只能在血泊中渐渐冰凉。

呵，有什么意思？你需要变强，而不是感情的寄托……

你怎么可以这样？！我已经想开了，我宁可永远地被人忘记，也不会让自己成为那些只能为后人叹惋的另一名以屠杀为生的凶兽。

我是zky啊！

我与你们，终究不一样！

这就是信仰！

Sun

不知怎么就想起来好久没有什么户外的活动了（我去，不至于把打架当成户外活动吧？！），趁着今天大家都闲着，不如出去爬山吧。

首都其实没什么高山，唯一有些名气的不过是离市中心不太远处的封神山，这个名字听说是由民间故事而得名，有点历史的传奇色彩，让我想到我的三次元意识（诶诶，zky，你就是这么称呼我的吗？！）读过的《风神录》（艹，那个叫《封神演义》，别听zky瞎扯）

封神山不算险峻，却依旧有几分奇美，我们面对着上山的小路，一时都不知道该怎么爬山了，别担心太多了，不就是来呼吸呼吸新鲜空气，放松一下看看大自然的吗？

阳光从掩映的绿叶间透过一条条光束，微微的雾气中如同一条条丝带，山上的风更加凉爽，一路上，我们没怎么说话，只是默默看着。城市在我们脚下远去，白云落在我们的头顶，终于到了山顶，大家纷纷散开，第一次在这样的高空眺望这个繁华的城市，我转过身，凝视着伫立着的一块巨石，拔出剑，在上面缓缓刻下自己都不明白的小诗：

远古之世吹来的风，载着传说奔向未来。

大地吮吸尽的鲜血，却早已被黄沙湮没。

豪情跌宕争锋虚妄，落泪成罪悔极莫追。

癫狂熔成坚韧铸铁，执念凝作易碎冰心。

Dream - LSS Short

This is part of the upcoming LSS 5 by XGN.

ONE

When we wake up from a dream, are we in reality or another dream?

White hall, I garb in the purest white clothes I could find, waiting for the “other one” to arrive. Deep breathing, in and out, I am quite nervous right now. This is my first time to wear like this and in a place like this, and I kinda hope it is the last time.

The guests are coming one by one. Soon, the hall is filled with people and therianthropes from different places. Since he has not appeared, I’d like to calm myself down with something, like, this book? Dream come true by Ventuswill senpai. “I hope I can get something to deal with the situation in this leaflet..” I thought.

My name is Doragon, an aqua dragon girl. Currently 495 years old, unmarried.

With the door sound, he comes, in a hurry. “Sorry, I am late. You didn’t get mad, did you?” He mopped the sweat from his brow, doing his best to manage it all.

“As everyone is here. We shall start.”

“Doragon, do you take to be your husband, to love, honor and cherish him forever more?”

Wait, what’s that? I’ve never heard I am going to do that. Actually, I don’t even know why I am here. “This must be a dream..” I thought,” so that means, I can do whatever I want?!?!”

“OF COURSE I DO~” After all, this is one of the happiest moments in my life, even though it is only a dream.

“Itaka, do you take to be your wife, to love, honor and cherish her forever more?”

“Yes, I do.”

This is going crazy!

“You may now kiss the bride”

It has been 3 hours. I should have been woken up… But this is such a lengthy dream, isn’t it?

……

The dream seems not to be ending. This is interesting. The wedding is over, but my mind is still a complete blank.

“Honey, what’s wrong with you? Why are you staring at the ceiling for long? Are you hungry? Are you being too nervous? Or you are not comfortable to be in this suit?”

Hungry? I am peckish though. Nervous? A bit afraid to wake up. Uncomfortable? Yes, I indeed feel painful in these stupid white clothes - they don’t fit, they are too small.

Wait. If I can feel the pain, it means it is not a dream, right? So that means…

I AM A NEWLYWED NOW!

I am glad he’s Itaka, the prince of the country. I don’t remember why he comes for me, but at least, I am a royal dragon now. I don’t have to hide from the public anymore!

TWO

Time flies. It has been nearly 15 years. Weirdly, 15 years seem like 1 second in my point of view. Maybe that’s the power of happiness.

We have got a child, Yuqi. A boy with draconic features and a cute human body. Today is his 10-year-old birthday. According to the custom, the boys are supposed to go out hunting for one day and one night alone. In this way, he will become more independent and braver.

“Take care!” I definitely want to accompany him, but my husband stops me from doing so. So instead, I stand there until I cannot see his back.

“Yuqi… That’s a weird name. Who gives him this name? I can’t remember anything now.” I whisper to myself, “This is such a weird storyline.”

“You are just too nervous. Just sleep until he comes back.” Itaka sensed my weirdness.

“That’s a good idea.”

THREE

………

Unnamed Plain, a boy is walking through the tallgrasses, holding a gun shaking in his hand. Nobody can be heard and nobody can hear him. “This is gonna be all right…” the boy whispers to himself.

“Wooof!” Wolf roaring can be heard in the distance. The boy starts to shake harder. Then, there are walking sounds all around him. “Who…are there?” Nobody answers, but the sound does not end. Poor child, he is lost in the tallgrass. He thought it was safe to stay because no one would notice him. But it turned out that he is the one who can’t see

Right now, he is surrounded by a herd of wolves. “Evil human, take my attack!” one wolf jumps up and gives him a bite. “Help! Help! Mama and daddy!” The boy shouts, even without realizing he was holding a gun. But it was too late, a wolf in human form, or werewolf, appears and takes his gun away,”it’s time to get our revenge!”

A gunshot can be heard in the distance. Another star appears in the sky.

FOUR

……

“What is that??????”Doragon wakes up, out of breath. “I’d better check out what’s going on”

Doragon opens her door. She is surprised. Itaka, her beloved husband, was lying on the ground, covered in blood. The whole castle, her room, was on fire. “!!??What happened when I was sleeping? Anyone help?”

“She is there!” some folks heard the shout and rushed towards Doragon. “Hugh, I am saved”, she thought. But again, to her surprise, they did not save her, but instead knock her out.

When Doragon is awoke, she finds herself tied to a cross. Her clothes have gone missing, leaving her naked. Many villagers can be seen. They are shouting: “Kill her! Kill her!”

“What did I do wrong?”Doragon shouted. “You evil dragon, shut up! It’s you and that evil king who made this kingdom like a hell!”

“What’s going on?” Doragon is confused, but nobody trusts her. The executioner appears. “Goodbye” With these words, he tears down her tail bloodily. “It’s time to end you bastard!” The crowds brust into cheers. The executioner takes out a knife, cuts open her chest, tears down her heart and shows it to the public.

FIVE

…….

“Mom, wake up! Today is father’s birthday!”

Oh? Who is that? Is it my “dead” son Yuqi?

I’ve had a bad dream, no, two bad dreams actually. What’s wrong with me?

“Oh? Really? Let’s prepare a cake for him, ok?” I try to pretend nothing wrong happened to me, giving him a smile.

It is so lucky that I am still alive after visiting so many hells.

But Doragon does not know, some angry folks have planned a fire outside the castle. Her second dream is happening again, but in reality… Or not?

SIX

……

“Doragon, time to wake up!” XGN shouts.

“Zzzzz. Just let me sleep..” Doragon says with unsatisfactory,”Wait??? THAT IS ALL A DREAM???? REALLY A LENGTHY DREAM???”

“What are you talking about? Time for heading to school!” XGN puts down his manga book.

“Alright…”

Is this reality? I hope it is not. Dream or no, it can only be a nightmare.

The End Of Chapter Dream

Dandelion - LSS Short

Repost from HHS Blog

This story is part of LSS4 by XGN.

Dandelion,dandelion,where will you fly? Will you take my heart away?

“I am feeling not good today” It’s late night, and Ookami the werewolf girl still could’t fall asleep. “Damn the math test.. It must be the test that makes me feel sick…”

“Damn, my head is getting heavier and heavier, I think.” Dizzily, our cute girl fell into sleep.

Don’t let… the hu..man catch.. us… cough…cough… Run!Run!Run…(Gunshot)

“Ahh!!!” Ookami was woken by the nightmare. “What kind of dream is that?” She thought, “Wait… This is not my house. Where am I?”

Looking around, she found herself in a place she never went before: white walls, white beds, white women… Isn’t this the hospital?

“You are finally awake.” A woman in dress said, smiling.

“Why I am in hospital?”

“At one night one of your neighbours complaint about your loud coughing. He thought you may have infected the flu COVID-99. After our examination, unfortunately, so you did.”

“Am… I going to die?”

“No. Please be confident. You will recover after several operations.”

“Operations??” Ookami thought, “That’s not nice. They will find out I am not a human… They will KILL me! They really caught me this time..”

“You seems still worried. Be confident, and we will help you recover! Believe in us! You can recover in at most 1 month.”

“Yey, I am going to die in 1 month” Ookami thought.

The nurse went out of the room, so Ookami decided to hang around. “A bed, a window, a desk, that’s all. How lonely I am in this one-man room.” Out the window, she couldn’t see anything because the room was too high. When she stopped talking, there was no sound in the room. Everything was just deadly quiet. The hospital, more like a white jail, trapped our girl inside.

Dandelion, dandelion, where will you go? Will you take me to see the mountain and the flow?

Listen, my girl. Your dear mom is coming to rescue you.. Just hang there! Wait, and you will be fine! Don’t be afraid, my little girl. “How.. do I know how long I will wait?..” “Just take this dendelion. When you know how much seeds are there, mom will come”

“What a dream is that?” Ookami woke with a huge dandelion in her hand, “To count how many seeds? It seems fun.”

“1st 2nd 3rd 4th…” The easy task wasn’t easy for our girl, for she was taken out several times to measure the temperature. It took her a long time, but she still couldn’t finish the task. The seeds were so much as if they magically regenerated.

Dandelion, dandelion, where will you stay? Can you help me get through this May?

Days have passed, weeks have passed. It’s time for our girl to have operations. Surprisingly, our girl didn’t feel worried during it, because all her mind was flooded by dandelion seeds. She knowed that, if she miscounted, her mom would never come.

“You are recovered and you can go now! Your friends are waiting for you at the gate!” One day, the nurse said so.

“Really? But I haven’t finished counting…” Ookami wanted to say, but she swallowed her remaining words. “OK!”

“You are finally recovered!” Over the gate, XGN, Doragon and Ninetails had been waiting for a long time.

“Hey, you guys? Do you know that how I passed through these days? My mom told me to count these…” Ookami was proudly sharing her “life experience”

After the story, the friends were amazed except XGN. He smiled and looked at this foolish girl. “She still didn’t know a thing.” XGN thought,”Her mother was shot dead on the day she was born. How could she come to save her?”

“oh by the way, how’s my Math test?” Ookami asked curiously.

“You? You failed it again!” XGN bursted into laughter

“Oh come on please!!! I tried so hard!!”

The End of Chapter Dandelion
