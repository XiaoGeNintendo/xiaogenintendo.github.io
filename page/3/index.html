本文由Zhuky执笔

Intro

大家好，我是zky，今天和XGN去了THO游玩，一早上完倪哥数学直接打车过去了（本来以为可以写一会儿作业，事实证明我太天真了），一路上很顺利，甚至没有出现我担心的找不到地方之类问题，车直接开进去停在了稻香音乐空间门口，名字起得不错，让我感到浓浓的风神录1面的味道~

整个活动场地给我的第一印象其实是“这么小？！”，不过没有让我失望，虽然有些挤，但是我们车万人却秩序井然（打游戏能在那么密集的弹幕中穿行自如，看来在三次元也依然如此啦！），在并不宽敞的空间里没有任何冲突。

关于摊位

大概逛了一圈摊位，主要出售以下几类：

  1. CD：让我万万没有想到的是，CD恐怕是一眼望去数量最多的。各种音乐社团的摊位上都摆满了CD，其实现在这个年代很少有人用光盘了，不过这种传统依然延续了下去。仔细想来，随手从网上下载几份音乐，和手中沉甸甸的一盒光盘，满足感绝对是不一样的。入手了一个50块的月华缭乱（那个长发小哥的音乐审美和我比较相似，做的音乐自然我会比较喜欢，试听了两首，毫不犹豫地买了 （编者注：二胡全套） 想要的话也许我可以把wav文件打包cowtransfer一下给大家，从百度网盘下载实在太慢了 （编者注：支持正版，打击盗版）

  2. 同人志/画册/本子：不得不说车万人可谓多才多艺，同人文章和画作真的可谓精致，有窗外一缕金色斜阳，洒满了生意盎然的小花坛，有少女眼中，闪烁着坚定的光芒，有刀锋银光一闪，剑气好似呼之欲出，也有红魔馆里，酌一杯美酒，于杯中细品的雅致。这种感觉实在是电脑屏幕上感受不到的，我和XGN大概花了将近150，买了4本画册（挺好的，各有特色，还有XGN喜欢的铁路元素） 编者注：还有某人喜欢的限制级内容

  3. 立牌/手办/画报：最常见的漫展商品，我个人比较喜欢立牌，比较方便携带，又很简约，主要考验立绘画师的美术功底和审美水平。鉴于立牌一般比较小，很难画出大量的细节，所以一般还是以可爱为主（所以有的就看上去比较憨憨，真的我觉得好的不多）。最让我耳目一新的设计是一个木板上一面是人物立绘，另一面是祝福语，然后我买了一个青蛙子和“长命百岁”（笑，不愧是我）

Zky：“这个图样图森破也敢往上面印吗？真的不怕FBI Open the door 然后查水表吗？”

摊主：“你看看这个呢？”

然后拿起青蛙子，翻到背面。

Zky：“啊！我就要这个了！”

摊主笑得脸都红了

  1. 卡牌游戏/电子游戏：不少由东方角色和特殊技能组成的游戏，唯一有一点可惜的是这些游戏绝大多数都停留在改编上，没有对于游戏类型，机制，玩法上的精彩创新，比如看到一个游戏，和炉石长得一模一样……没有买，我要打的游戏已经排队成山了，卡牌游戏……一盒180还不知道好不好玩，我还是别赌了

音乐会

整个活动最精彩的就是现场音乐会了，那个我比较喜欢的长发小哥是第一个上场的，很能带动气氛，二胡拉得很好，会讲段子，很“记仇”（其实很幽默的哈！）

“大家想听什么曲子呀？”明显是觉得自己啥东方曲都会拉。

“二泉映月！”

“各位壮汉往那边靠拢一下——大家帮帮忙……拉出去！”

然而并没有壮汉出现（笑）

两首曲子演奏完之后，开始推销自己的商品：

“这两首曲子被收录在我们的新专辑‘整活’里，还有最后9张，其中有两张是坏的，请大家冲去购买的时候请注意秩序，不要发生踩踏事故，除了那个喊‘二泉映月’的，他的话…你们尽管踩~”

他是那种特别会带气氛，特出趟的那种小伙子（对我算是个好榜样），他的两首曲子我都很喜欢，超级好评。

后面还有3个乐团（其实也就是每个社团的一两个代表上去唱），总体上都是很不错的，虽然从音质上来说，肯定没有录音棚里能调音能修正的那种来得好，但是现场效果无疑绝佳。我zky只能感叹如果有一天我也能唱那么好，那就很满足了。

无何有之乡是后面几首曲子中最喜欢的，旋律主要是妖妖梦的原曲，填词是最棒的，有那么一点中二气息，恍惚又细品出几分轮回幻灭，悠闲的意境中不乏内心感叹。

其他也有几首不错的，奈何本人没文化，日语一出一窍不通，没能很好地体会其中内涵。

我全程手机录制视频，一首曲子3到5分钟，我录了大概8首（还是9首？） （编者注：9首），中途还不小心摁到录制完毕键（真的控制不住累到抽筋的手指），不过手机相机实在是太好了，视频完全没有模糊和抖动。我和XGN没有挤在最前排的人群中，我们在后面有一个可以上去的二楼观看。

吐槽一下

  1. 座位几乎没有，全程直挺挺地站着感觉梦回军训

  2. 怎么这么闷热啊！我中途至少出去放了两次风

午饭

后来转了转，我们去吃午饭（喊饿的是我，最后XGN吃完了半天，吃饱了还没吃完的也是我），餐厅可真良心，在这种地段一份饭价格居然还是三十几，本以为景点价绝对5字起步的。鸡排有一点油腻，不过花茶很解腻，口感清爽，很好地弥补了这一点。饭里还有黄瓜（不知道为什么，被切成了大波浪薯片的形状，感觉这么做很麻烦啊？），看来是很用心了。

餐厅偶遇空洞骑士玩家，我就目瞪口呆地看着他怒屠神居五门，高端剑技玩家，无护符法术辅助输出，有辐辉级经验，超过80%成就收集，感觉再玩玩就是可以钢魂亡怒流的神仙了。我膜拜了一下，欣赏了这平时只能在视频里才能看到的场面。（我说车万人的技术力这么强的吗？随便一个双厨就……）

快吃完的时候注意到一个走出去的大哥穿着我们学校的校服裤子，XGN说很多裤子都长这样，不过我还是去和人家拾答了一下，果然是我们学校的，国际部，高三，把我拉进一个我们学校的车万群，看他的包上面都是“东方星莲船”“9961”等车万经典字样，估计也是老车万人了。

其实140元门票中包含的午餐就是一汉堡，然而看似并不抵饱，我就看着某些壮汉一边丢了汉堡空盒子一边走向旁边的餐厅。

我本来考虑午饭的时候并不知道那里还有饭店，不过当时看到会场可以任意多次进出我就放心了，这种地方虽然人气不算旺，但要点个外卖还是绝对没有问题的，所以说嘛，吃饭这种事情还是要掌握在自己手上的（笑）

一点感想

总体来说，THO是一个很棒的车万人大聚会，就是越共回了大本营那种感觉，身边的人都是车车人，音乐也是车万音乐，你再也不用担心脑海中崩出精彩的梗却无人听懂，同样的，身边人聊天的内容都会给你一种亲切感，且不说东方本身是什么，是STG，是番还是ACG圈，但她成为了我们共同的文化，成为了我们内心远离现实的那一面上，共同的美好信仰。来到这里，如同伸出手来，将平时远在天边，只能透过屏幕冰冷的微光去静观的事物，握在了自己温热的手心。

一年到头，总要伸个懒腰，打个哈欠，说笑两句~

Afterlife - LSS Short

Intro

I hate writing long articles, so I will make the story shorter.

Oh right, LSS does not exist anymore. Then it means, this world will be forgotten and removed soon. Don’t feel bad, it’s only a branch after all! Read Further Reading: How to know my OC first, won’t you?

ONE

The wheels of fortune,

going round and round.

Carrying greetings and farewells, happiness and sorrow, birth and death.

When it halts finally,

Everything will be silence.

……

No one knows why, no one knows how, but Ninetail is dead. There’s a rumor saying that she was murdered. But no one can confirm it anymore.

“…… As the most beloved maid for Miss Ninetail. I feel extremely guilty and sad. I can’t protect her.”

“However, as she always have told me: I have to live on my own. So, in order to follow her after-death order, I, Hikari are determined to live independently!”

“……”

That’s Hikari. She speaks with eyes of fire of enthusiastic and a holding fist. Unlike those weak creatures like Ookami, XGN and Doragon, she seems not to be dropping any tears, even not a sense of sadness. Maybe that’s how she was trained as a maid at the first place?

“Hikari, that’s optimistic of you to say that~ I was moved by that speech.” said XGN, sobbing quietly, “I believe you can do it! You are PROFESSIONAL, after all!”

“Believe me! I will still treat you like my ex-master!”

Wagging her tail, pointing out her fingers at XGN, smiling as her, this is our adorable and affectionate Hikari.

TWO

House.

Ring, ring

Knock, knock

“Master XGN, it’s time to get up~ Time for a new school day~” Behind the doors was the gentle voice of Hikari. It’s not hard to imagine a dragon in its human form knocking with a big smile.

“Today’s breakfast is sweet bean bread and my speciality, cheese cake!”

On the table there were glasses of milk with a little steam coming out, golden brown bread with porcelain plates holding perfect and charming cakes. What’s more, a glorious red sheet was holding all of these things, making it more appetizing.

“Wow! That’s, that’s, far more than LUXURIOUS!” shouted Doragon.

“It tastes good too!”

“Hey! Don’t eat so fast!”

“How much time does it take to make all these?”

“…..”

“If Miss Ninetail were here, she would be really amazed at your food! I really hope she could enjoy this!” XGN looked at Hikari, who had been standing back waiting. He wanted to cheer her up, apparently.

“Thank you for appreciation!”

Thus, she watched XGN and his classmates left.

“So what shall I do now?” Hikari sat down, began combing her scales on tail, “When will Miss Ninetail be back?”

“So they REALLY thought Miss Ninetail died, how stupid.” giggled Hikari, “Don’t they realize that last time Miss Ninetail ‘died’, she returned only in 2 weeks? And, how could such a strong and god-like fox die with no clue?”

Outside, there was noise from cars, roaring down the street. Inside, there was our Hikari, sitting quietly.

THREE

Kitchen.

“So, this should do it.”

After nicely covered the cake with aluminum foil, Hikari put it inside the fridge.

“If Miss Ninetail comes back early, she will be able to enjoy this food without waiting~”

“And, how about I clean the room for her? It will be a BIG surprise~”, giggled Hikari, again.

After a hour’s work or less, Ninetail’s room became as tidy as new, shining in the sunlight.

“Report: Miss Ninetail may be back in any minute, so I have to be ready! And I shall not let her friends down, or she will blame me when she comes back! Exp. Date: 12/19.”

……

“Maybe I should keep two cakes. Everyone needs to eat after all”

Hikari put another cake into the fridge.

“Today I will clear her wardrobe too!”

Passionate, lovely as her, this is our Hikari.

……

One day, two days, three days. Then there was seven leaving and greeting.

Soon, the fridge was full of dim sum and desserts of different types. The house had been cleaned 10 or 100 times.

“Calm down, Hikari.. You are being TOO passionate. Don’t push yourself too hard.” said XGN with an embarassed face.

“OK… I will try not to overdo it… I will only do what you say from now on.”

Two weeks, three weeks, and Christmas came. However, there was no symbol that Ninetail would be back.

The cakes in fridge had turned bad, there was dust covering Ninetail’s room again.

“Report: Nothing today. Exp. Date: 12/25”

“Report: Nothing today. Exp. Date: 12/26”

“Report: Nothing today. Exp. Date: 12/30”

“Report: Nothing today. Exp. Date: 1/1”

“……”

This is our Hikari, who gets bored easily for doing nothing.

FOUR

It’s 1st Jan finally.

Living room.

“Hey! Do you have any questions to ask me today? I can PREDICT your future and fortune for the next year! But one question only, OK?” said XGN proudly.

XGN knows almost everything that happened in the past and would take place in the future.

“When will Miss Ninetail be back?” whispered Hikari to XGN. She wasn’t that enthusiastic and happy as December now, it seemed.

“Hasn’t she passed away, about 1 month ago? You should know that.”

“Oh..? Aren’t you pretendi..? Sorry… Thank you very much.”

Hikari gave XGN a big hug, nearly making him suffocate.

FIVE

Living Room. 2nd Jan.

“See you, my friends~”

“See you afternoon!”

Sighed, and again. After sending them off, Hikari opened the door which she hadn’t touched in half a month.

The bed made a very loud noise, but she climbed on. dipping feather in the ink, as she wrote:

A contract can make a life.It can also leave a wanderer.

She laid on bed, with a glass bottle in her hand.

“Sorry. This is farewell……? I hope it’s not……?”

This is our Hikari. Lying peacefully and silently, sparkling in the warm sunlight.

With an eternal smile as she, with a dream and goal like this, this is the greatest happiness one can have, in her standard.

Orange Boy Can You Solve It Out? Ep.42

思考题 with anime again

Spicy Wolf

XGN have watched the famous anime Spice and Wolf and now as usual, he will propose a problem.

In the country, there are N cities and M bidirectional roads. Each road connects two city and has a dangerous point Di. Each city has three properties: Pi - the profit, Fi - the deliciousness of the food there and Gi - the number of attractions in the city.

Now Lawrence, Horo and Nora are at city 1. Before the trip, Lawrence will give Nora X coins, which will be determined by you. This will make all roads with dangerous points less than or equal to X be available for passing.

Their journey will last for T days. In each day, they will first move to an adjacent city. There Lawrence will do some business and get Pi coins. The way they determine which city to go will follow these rules:

  • Assume they are at city x and all adjacent cities are in adj
  • Horo will first choose an adjacent city i randomly with the probability
  • Then Nora will choose an adjacent city j randomly with the probability
  • Then among city i and j, they will go to the one with higher profit.

For example, if the adjacent cities have [{P,F,G}]=[{1,2,3},{2,3,4},{5,6,7}], then a possible process is:

  • Horo chose city 1
  • Nora chose city 3
  • Lawrence decided to go to city 3 because it had higher profit
  • Possibility of this scene:

What’s the maximum expected profit after day T when the initial X is optimal? Please print the profit and the initial X.

Constraints

For all tests,

The size of the set of all Di is no bigger than 50.

All Pi are different

Hint

Brute force X, calculate expected value adjacent matrix, do matrix fastpow on it.

Allow me to cut through those words!

Have you ever played Danganronpa? It’s a game which allows you to cut through others words by exposing lies. Our adorable swordsman Ookami decides to try it too!

There are N words Ookami needs to cut. They will appear in a plane as rectangles. The i-th one will appear at second , with its left-up corner at and right-down corner at . The left-up corner will move to and its right-down corner will move to linearly and smoothly in seconds. It will also have a health of

In every moment, Ookami can choose one of the following lines and damage all rectangles that intersect with it(include boundaries) and doing this will cost her 1 energy.

  • y=x
  • x=0
  • y=0
  • y=-x

What’s the minimal number of energy Ookami has to spend to kill all rectangles?

Please note that:

  • A rectangle will die when its health is 0
  • Ookami can cut multiple times in a single moment even with different lines.
  • She doesn’t need to cut them at integer seconds.

It works by simply setting the background every 1 second and runs in the background.

Tested on Windows7(32bit) and Windows10(64bit)

You can stop the program by killing it in task manager or rebooting.
