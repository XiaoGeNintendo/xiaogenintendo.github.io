Formally, you are given string s,t,e.

You can repeat each letter in s k times where k is a number chosen by you. For example, if s=”abbc” and k=3, s=”aaabbbbbbccc”

Then you can append as many e as you want.

Is it possible to make t a substring of your big string?

Example

1
s=”abc” e=”d” t=”bbccdd”
Output=YES
Explain:
choose k=2, then s=”aabbcc”
then append two “d” then s=”aabbccdd”
2
s=”abcde” e=”” t=”bbccdde”
Output=NO

Constriants

Subtask 1(100%): 0<=|e|<=100,000 1<=|s|,|t|<=100,000
