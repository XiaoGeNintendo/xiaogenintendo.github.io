Electric Heritage

Haniyasushin Keiki loves crafting. She has kinds of items. The i-th kind of item has name . She also has recipes. The i-th recipe needs of different kinds of different items as input. The j-th item is and needs of them. Then the recipe will produce of number . Note that the recipe is not bidirectional. Kitcho Yachie now has kinds of items. The i-th kind of item is and has of it. She now wonders what is the maximum number of a certain kind of item she can make? You need to print the solution.

Example

Recipes: 1 log -> 4 plank 2 plank -> 4 stick 3 plank + 2 stick -> 1 pickaxe Have: 1 log 4 plank Dest: pickaxe Output: 2 1 log -> 4 plank 2 plank -> 4 stick 3 plank + 2 stick -> 1 pickaxe 3 plank + 2 stick -> 1 pickaxe

Constriants

Subtask 1(49%): always holds Subtask 2(49%): ${x_i}j=1n,m\leq10\sum{j=1}^{len_i}{x_i}_j\leq9,y_i\leq9n\leq10^5m\leq10^5$ 把钱汇组长就是她！
