How to know my OC

How to know my OC / 如何了解我（or ZKY）的原创人物

中文在文章底部。

First you should know that most characters in my universe and Segicverse are the same. Also, history contains mainline story and branch story. Branch stories are used to show personalities and interesting facts about a certain characters while mainline stories are stories that really happened.

Currently, you can access to my world in the follow way:

Method Story type Author Link
Roaming Travellers in Fantasy Extravaganza Official XGN On this site
Segicverse Official Zhuky.RLG https://www.bilibili.com/read/cv5224967
LSS Short Branch XGN On this site
LSS Problemset Branch XGN Dead

Also, you can see OC (rarely) from Hell Hole Studios Games. They are all branch stories. You can search them on HHS Project

TestSTG 3 Kazami Ryoryu*, Nanjin Yatsujitera*, Hikari^
