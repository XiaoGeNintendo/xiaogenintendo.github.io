    因为hellholestudios.club即将过期，我们决定启用新的域名hellholestudios.top，所有hellholestudios网站域名均使用hellholestudios.top域名

Due to the domain “hellholestudios.club” is expiring, we decide to use the new domain “hellholestudios.top”. All the websites of hellholestudios will use the “hellholestudios.top” domain.

现使用hellholestudios.club访问会自动跳转到hellholestudios.top，到10月5日.club域名将停止解析，届时只能使用hellholestudios.top访问，请各位悉知

Now visiting hellholestudios.club domains will be forwarded to hellholestudios.top automatically. The hellholestudios.club domain will stop using after 5th Oct. You can only use “hellholestudios.top” domain to visit after that.

谢谢！
